Section 3 Training Instructions

In Section 3 your task will be the same as in Section 1: in each round, you will be randomly paired with another player, and then asked to select an action.
Each player will be the same color as they were throughout Section 2.
As before, the outcome of each round will depend on both players' chosen actions.

There are two differences relative to Section 1:

Actions

Each player will have more than two actions to choose from.
The actions will be represented by numbers instead of letters:
1, 2, 3, ... replace U and D
1, 2, 3, ... replace L and R

Probabilities

Your probability of receiving a ${{Constants.payoff_info.uc.action.amount}} prize will be 30% plus the probability from the chosen cell of the matrix.
For example:
If your number in the chosen cell is 20, your prize probability will be 30% + 20% = 50%.
If your number in the chosen cell is -10, your prize probability will be 30% + (-10%) = 20%.

All matrix numbers are between -30 and 70, so your prize probabilty is guaranteed to be between 0% and 100%.



To make sure you understand these changes, you will now play a training round, similar to that which you played before Section 1.
You will be asked to answer a question about a payoff matrix by typing a whole number into a box.
Once you have typed the correct number, a 'Continue' button will appear, which you may click.
If you have any questions, please raise your hand and wait for assistance.
Otherwise, click 'Continue' to proceed to the training round.
